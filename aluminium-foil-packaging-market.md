<p><p>The aluminium foil packaging market comprises three main types: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible packaging. Rigid aluminium packaging includes containers and trays that offer robust protection for products, making it ideal for food and pharmaceuticals. Semi-rigid packaging incorporates a combination of rigidity and flexibility, often used for items like blister packs. Flexible packaging is lightweight and adaptable, commonly used for pouches and wraps, providing excellent barrier properties and convenience, making it popular in various consumer goods sectors.</p></p>
