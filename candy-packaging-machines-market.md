<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Candy packaging machines are categorized mainly into vertical and horizontal markets based on their packaging orientation. Vertical packaging machines utilize a vertical format, efficiently filling and sealing bags from the top down, ideal for small, lightweight candies. Conversely, horizontal packaging machines operate in a horizontal format, suitable for larger or bulk items, enabling side-sealing and flow-wrapping. Both market types cater to different production needs and packaging styles, enhancing efficiency and maintaining product quality in the confectionery industry.</p></p>
