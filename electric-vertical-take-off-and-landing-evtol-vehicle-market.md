<p><p>Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing (eVTOL) vehicles are advanced aerial mobility solutions designed for urban and regional transport. The **Vector Thrust Type** uses tilting rotors or engines to transition between vertical lift and horizontal flight, offering efficient cruising. **Cruising eVTOLs** focus on sustained forward flight, optimizing range and speed for longer distances. **Multi-Rotor eVTOLs** rely on multiple fixed rotors for vertical lift and hover, excelling in stability and maneuverability for short-range urban missions. Each type caters to specific use cases, balancing efficiency, range, and operational flexibility.</p></p>
