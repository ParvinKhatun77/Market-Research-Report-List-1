<p><strong>The Recirculating Cooling Water System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Recirculating Cooling Systems</li><li>Closed Recirculating Cooling Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recirculating cooling water system market is primarily divided into two types: open and closed recirculating cooling systems. Open recirculating systems draw water from a source, typically using a cooling tower to dissipate heat, and then return warmed water to the source, making it susceptible to evaporation and contamination. Closed recirculating systems, on the other hand, use a closed-loop mechanism that minimizes evaporation and contamination by recycling water through heat exchangers, offering greater efficiency and reduced water usage.</p></p>
