<p><p>The elastomers market comprises various polymer types, each with distinct properties and applications. SBR (Styrene Block Copolymers) offers excellent resilience, while IIR (Butyl Elastomer) is known for its airtightness and weather resistance. NBR (Nitrile Elastomer) provides exceptional oil resistance. ACM (Acrylic Elastomer) is suited for high-temperature applications. EPM (Ethylene-Propylene Elastomer) offers flexibility and chemical resistance. PEBA (Thermoplastic Polyether Block Amides) combines elasticity and durability. SBC (Styrene Block Copolymers) ensures stability in tough conditions. TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) merges thermoplastics' processability with rubber's performance. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethanes) is known for its versatility, while TPV (Thermoplastic Vulcanizates) blends rubber-like elasticity with thermoplastic processing.</p></p>
