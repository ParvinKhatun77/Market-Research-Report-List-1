<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water purification flocculant market is typically categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I flocculants usually consist of organic polymers, effective for removing suspended solids and enhancing sedimentation in various water treatment processes. In contrast, Type II flocculants are primarily inorganic compounds, such as aluminum or iron salts, which aid in coagulation and flocculation by destabilizing particles in water. Together, these types serve diverse applications, ensuring cleaner water for consumption and industrial use.</p></p>
