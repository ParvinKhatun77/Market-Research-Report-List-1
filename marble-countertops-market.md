<p><ul><li>Artificial</li><li>Natural</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marble countertops are available in two primary market types: artificial and natural. Natural marble countertops are crafted from quarried stone, offering unique veining and patterns, prized for their elegance and durability. They are often more expensive due to sourcing and processing costs. Artificial marble countertops, made from engineered materials like resin and pigments, mimic natural marble's appearance but are more affordable, consistent in design, and resistant to stains. Both cater to different preferences, balancing aesthetics, cost, and functionality.</p></p>
