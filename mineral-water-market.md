<p><p>The mineral water market is primarily divided into two types: natural mineral water and man-made mineral water. Natural mineral water is sourced from underground springs and contains various beneficial minerals, often marketed for health benefits. In contrast, man-made mineral water is created through the artificial addition of minerals to purified water, allowing for customization of mineral content. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with natural mineral water appealing to those seeking authenticity and health benefits, while man-made options attract those looking for consistency and tailored flavors.</p></p>
