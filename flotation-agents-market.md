<p><ul><li>Collector</li><li>Foaming Agent</li><li>Regulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flotation agents market comprises three main types: collectors, foaming agents, and regulators. Collectors are chemicals that enhance the attachment of minerals to air bubbles, facilitating their separation from the slurry. Foaming agents stabilize the bubbles, increasing the efficiency of the flotation process. Regulators are additives that control the pH and modify the surface properties of minerals, optimizing the overall flotation conditions. Together, these agents play a crucial role in mineral processing and extraction in various industries.</p></p>
