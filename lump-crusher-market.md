<p><p>The lump crusher market can be categorized into two primary types: dual-rotor and single-rotor systems, along with other specialized variants. Dual-rotor lump crushers utilize two rotors driving intermeshing blades, efficiently breaking down large lumps into smaller particles, ideal for high-capacity applications. Single-rotor lump crushers employ a single rotating shaft with hammers or knives, suitable for lighter-duty tasks. Other types include customized solutions that cater to specific industry requirements, enhancing versatility in material processing across sectors.</p></p>
