<p><p>The flexible electrical conduits market comprises two primary types: metal and non-metal conduits. Metal conduits, often made from materials like aluminum or steel, offer superior durability, protection against physical damage, and resistance to harsh environmental conditions. They are commonly used in industrial and outdoor settings. Non-metal conduits, including PVC and fiberglass, are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for indoor applications where flexibility and ease of installation are essential. Both types cater to various electrical installation needs, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
