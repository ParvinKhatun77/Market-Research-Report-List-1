<p><ul><li>Batch</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial frying system market is primarily divided into batch and continuous types. Batch frying systems cook food in discrete quantities, allowing for flexibility and control over the cooking process, which is ideal for small to medium-scale operations. In contrast, continuous frying systems operate seamlessly, providing a steady flow of food through the fryer, enhancing efficiency and scalability for large-scale production. Each type caters to different operational needs, balancing factors like production volume, product variety, and energy consumption.</p></p>
