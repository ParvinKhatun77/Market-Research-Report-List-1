<p><p>Mobile payment technology encompasses various market types, each facilitating transactions in distinct ways. Proximity payments allow users to pay by tapping devices nearby. Near Field Communication (NFC) enables seamless data exchange at short ranges. QR code payments involve scanning codes to initiate payments. Remote payments facilitate transactions over the internet. SMS-based payments utilize text messaging, while USSD/STK allows interactive codes for transactions. Direct operator billing simplifies purchases via mobile carrier accounts, and digital wallets store payment information for convenient access.</p></p>
