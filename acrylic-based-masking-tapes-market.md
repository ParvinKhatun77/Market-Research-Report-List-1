<p><p>The Acrylic-based Masking Tapes market is divided into two primary types: single-sided and double-sided tapes. Single-sided tapes feature adhesive on one side, ideal for general masking, surface protection, and painting tasks, allowing easy application and removal. In contrast, double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, offering superior bonding for mounting and securing materials without visible tape. Each type serves distinct applications in construction, automotive, and DIY projects, catering to various user needs and preferences in adhesive solutions.</p></p>
