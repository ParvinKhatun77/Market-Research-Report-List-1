<p><p>The heart stent market comprises various types catering to different patient needs. Drug-eluting stents release medication to prevent restenosis, promoting long-term patency. Bare metal stents provide basic support without drug release, suitable for specific cases. Bioactive stents incorporate bioactive materials to enhance healing and integration with the artery wall. Radioactive stents use radiation to inhibit cell proliferation, reducing restenosis risk. Each type offers unique benefits, targeting diverse clinical scenarios and patient conditions in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
