<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Double Column Machining Center Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Beam Type</li><li>Moving Beam Type</li><li>Moving Column Type</li><li>Crane Type</li><li>Compound Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double column machining center market comprises several types differentiated by their structural designs. The fixed beam type features a stationary beam with a movable table, offering stability and precision. The moving beam type allows the beam to traverse on a fixed column, enhancing flexibility. The moving column type has a mobile column with a fixed table, optimizing space and accessibility. Crane type centers utilize a crane-like structure for versatile machining, while compound type centers combine features of various designs for enhanced functionality.</p></p>
