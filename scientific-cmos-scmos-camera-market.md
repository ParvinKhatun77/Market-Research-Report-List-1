<p><strong>The Scientific CMOS (sCMOS) Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Illuminated</li><li>Back Illuminated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Scientific CMOS (sCMOS) camera market is categorized into Front Illuminated (FI) and Back Illuminated (BI) types. Front Illuminated sCMOS cameras feature a traditional sensor design, where light must pass through metal wiring, resulting in lower sensitivity and dynamic range. In contrast, Back Illuminated sCMOS cameras have a design that optimizes light capture by reversing the sensor structure, allowing for increased sensitivity, improved noise performance, and enhanced dynamic range, making them ideal for low-light applications in scientific imaging.</p></p>
