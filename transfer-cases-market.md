<p><p>Transfer cases are essential components in four-wheel-drive and all-wheel-drive vehicles, distributing power between front and rear axles. The market is divided into two main types: gear-driven and chain-driven transfer cases. Gear-driven transfer cases use interlocking gears for power transfer, offering durability and strength, ideal for heavy-duty and off-road applications. Chain-driven transfer cases use a metal chain for power distribution, providing smoother operation, lighter weight, and better fuel efficiency, making them suitable for on-road and light-duty vehicles. Both types cater to different vehicle needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
