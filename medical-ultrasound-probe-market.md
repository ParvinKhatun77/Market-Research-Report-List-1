<p><p>The medical ultrasound probe market is categorized into several types based on their design and application. Linear type probes produce high-resolution images, ideal for superficial structures. Convex type probes have a curved array, providing a wider field of view for abdominal scans. Phased array type probes enable imaging in tight spaces like the heart, offering high fidelity. Endocavitary probes are designed for internal examinations, such as transvaginal or transrectal imaging. Other types include specialty probes for niche applications.</p></p>
