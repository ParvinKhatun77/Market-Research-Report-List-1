<p><p>The aquaculture shade net market is categorized based on shade coefficients, which determine the level of sunlight filtration. Nets with a shade coefficient of less than 0.3 allow significant sunlight, promoting plant growth while minimizing heat stress. Those with a coefficient of 0.5-0.75 provide moderate shade, balancing light and temperature for optimal aquatic environments. Nets with a coefficient above 0.75 offer high shading, useful in preventing algae growth and protecting sensitive species from excessive sunlight and heat.</p></p>
