<p><p>Behavioral biometrics refers to the technology that analyzes patterns in human behavior to verify identity. This method enhances security measures by adding an additional layer of authentication, making it more difficult for unauthorized users to gain access.</p><p>The Behavioral Biometrics Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing concerns over cybersecurity and the rising demand for advanced authentication methods.
