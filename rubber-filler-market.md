<p><p>The rubber filler market comprises two main types: organic fillers and inorganic fillers. Organic fillers, derived from natural sources like plant fibers or synthetic polymers, enhance rubber properties while reducing weight and cost. In contrast, inorganic fillers, such as carbon black and silica, improve strength, durability, and thermal stability. Both types play crucial roles in manufacturing rubber products, offering benefits like enhanced performance, reduced production costs, and improved environmental sustainability, catering to diverse industry applications.</p></p>
