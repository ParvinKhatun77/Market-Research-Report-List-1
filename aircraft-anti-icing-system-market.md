<p><ul><li>Pneumatic De-icing Boots</li><li>Electro-thermal</li><li>Electro-mechanical</li><li>TKS Ice Protection</li><li>Bleed Air</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Anti-icing System market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Pneumatic De-icing Boots** use inflatable rubber membranes that expand to break off ice. </p><p>2. **Electro-thermal systems** employ heated surfaces to melt ice. </p><p>3. **Electro-mechanical systems** utilize mechanical devices for ice removal. </p><p>4. **TKS Ice Protection** disperses a fluid that prevents ice accumulation. </p><p>5. **Bleed Air systems** channel hot air from engines to surface areas. </p><p>6. **Passive systems** rely on aircraft design and surface coatings to minimize ice formation.</p></p>
