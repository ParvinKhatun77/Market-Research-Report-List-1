<p><p>Electrically Tunable Focusing Lenses (ETFL) are advanced optical devices that can change their focal length by applying an electrical signal. The market includes types such as Flat Convex and Flat Concave lenses. Flat Convex lenses are designed to converge light, making them ideal for applications requiring magnification and focus control, while Flat Concave lenses diverge light, which is useful for beam expansion and imaging systems. These technologies enhance flexibility in various fields, including telecommunications, imaging, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
