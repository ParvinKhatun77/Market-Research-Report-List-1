<p><p>The SOI Silicon on Insulator Wafer market comprises two main types: Thick SOI Wafers and Thin SOI Wafers. Thick SOI Wafers, typically exceeding 100 micrometers in silicon layer thickness, are used in power devices and RF applications due to their high electrical performance and thermal stability. In contrast, Thin SOI Wafers, usually measuring less than 100 micrometers, are favored for advanced semiconductor technologies, including CMOS devices and radiofrequency applications, due to their reduced parasitic capacitance and better scalability.</p></p>
