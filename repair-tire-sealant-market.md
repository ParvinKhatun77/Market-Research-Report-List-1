<p><p>The repair tire sealant market is primarily divided into two types: gel and aerosol. Gel sealants are thick, viscous compounds that provide a durable seal by filling punctures in tires, often preferred for their longer-lasting effects and ease of application. In contrast, aerosol sealants are packaged in pressurized containers for quick and convenient use; they spray a sealant compound directly into the tire, allowing for immediate repair while on the go. Both types cater to different user preferences and situations.</p></p>
