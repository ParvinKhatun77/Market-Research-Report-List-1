<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) stabilizers market includes various types designed to protect materials from UV degradation. Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) prevent photo-oxidation and enhance durability. UV absorbers absorb harmful UV radiation and dissipate it as heat, protecting polymers. Quenchers act by neutralizing excited states of molecules, thereby preventing photodegradation. Other market segments include additives that combine multiple functions, enhancing the overall performance and longevity of materials exposed to sunlight, crucial in industries like plastics, coatings, and textiles.</p></p>
