<p><p>The zero-emission aircraft market encompasses three primary types: hydrogen aircraft, electric aircraft, and solar aircraft. Hydrogen aircraft utilize fuel cells or combustion engines powered by hydrogen, emitting only water vapor. Electric aircraft rely on batteries to propel their engines, aiming for reduced emissions and noise pollution. Solar aircraft harness solar energy through photovoltaic cells to power their systems, often used in smaller models. Together, these technologies represent innovative solutions to reduce aviation's environmental impact while promoting sustainable air travel.</p></p>
