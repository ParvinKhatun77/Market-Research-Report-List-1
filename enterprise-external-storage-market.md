<p><p>The Enterprise External Storage Market is primarily segmented into All Flash Arrays (AFA) and Hybrid Flash Arrays (HFA). AFA utilizes only flash storage, offering high speed and performance, making it ideal for data-intensive applications. In contrast, HFA combines flash and traditional hard disk drives (HDD), balancing cost and performance for diverse workloads. This allows organizations to optimize their storage infrastructure, leveraging the speed of flash for critical tasks while utilizing HDD for less demanding data, enhancing overall efficiency and capacity.</p></p>
