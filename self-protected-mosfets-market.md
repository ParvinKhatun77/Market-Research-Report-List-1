<p><strong>The Self-Protected MOSFETs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Side Devices</li><li>High Side Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-protected MOSFETs are categorized into low side and high side devices based on their placement in a circuit. Low side MOSFETs are positioned between the load and ground, providing easy control and limiting current flow to protect against faults. High side MOSFETs are located between the power supply and the load, offering better efficiency and circuit protection. Both types feature built-in protection mechanisms to prevent damage from overvoltage, overcurrent, or thermal stress, thereby enhancing system reliability.</p></p>
