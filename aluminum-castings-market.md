<p><strong>The Aluminum Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum castings market is primarily segmented into two types: die casting and permanent mold casting. Die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold under high pressure, resulting in precision components with smooth surfaces, making it ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, permanent mold casting uses reusable molds and employs gravity or low pressure to fill them, allowing for better structural integrity and surface finish. Both methods cater to various industries, emphasizing efficiency and quality in aluminum component manufacturing.</p></p>
