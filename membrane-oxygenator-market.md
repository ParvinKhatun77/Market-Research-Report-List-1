<p><ul><li>Flat Sheet Membrane Oxygenator</li><li>Hollow Fibre Membrane Oxygenator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Membrane Oxygenator market consists of Flat Sheet and Hollow Fibre types. Flat Sheet Membrane Oxygenators utilize a larger surface area for gas exchange, offering efficient oxygenation but often requiring a more substantial footprint. Conversely, Hollow Fibre Membrane Oxygenators feature numerous tiny fibers that enhance surface area while maintaining a compact design, promoting effective oxygen transfer in a smaller space. Both types are essential in cardiopulmonary bypass and extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) procedures, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
