<p><p>The light-curing adhesives market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a primary solvent, promoting environmental sustainability with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs). They offer quick curing times and are ideal for various substrates. Conversely, solvent-based adhesives rely on organic solvents to achieve adhesion, providing robust bonding strength and versatility across industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs, balancing performance, environmental considerations, and application methods in diverse industries.</p></p>
