<p><p>The Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Management Market includes various types of devices designed to regulate the flow and pressure of CSF in patients with neurological conditions. CSF shunts are implanted systems that redirect CSF from the brain to another body cavity, preventing buildup and relieving pressure. CSF drainage devices, on the other hand, facilitate the removal of excess fluid from the cranial cavity, often used in cases of injury or infection. Together, these tools are crucial in managing CSF-related disorders.</p></p>
