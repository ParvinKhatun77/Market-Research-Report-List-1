<p><strong>The UV LED for Sterilization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-A LED</li><li>UV-B LED</li><li>UV-C LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV LED sterilization market is segmented into three primary types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs, with wavelengths around 320-400 nm, are primarily used for curing applications and are less effective for sterilization. UV-B LEDs, emitting light around 280-320 nm, have some germicidal properties but are not the main choice for disinfection. UV-C LEDs, operating at 100-280 nm, are the most effective for sterilization, capable of inactivating bacteria and viruses, making them preferred for various hygiene applications.</p></p>
