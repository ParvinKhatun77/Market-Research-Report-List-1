<p><p>The assistive technologies market for visual impairment encompasses several key types. Braille displays convert digital text into Braille, enabling users to read electronically. Note takers facilitate the input and organization of information in Braille or text. Magnifiers enhance visibility of printed materials, while Braille printers and embossers produce tactile reading materials. Braille writers allow users to create Braille documents manually. Together, these technologies enhance accessibility and independence for individuals with visual impairments, improving their quality of life.</p></p>
