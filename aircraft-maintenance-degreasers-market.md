<p><ul><li>Water-based Degreasers</li><li>Solvent-based Degreasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft maintenance degreasers market is primarily divided into two types: water-based degreasers and solvent-based degreasers. Water-based degreasers utilize water as a primary solvent, making them environmentally friendly and safe for various surfaces. They effectively remove grease and contaminants with minimal health risks. In contrast, solvent-based degreasers employ chemical solvents to dissolve grease, offering strong cleaning power for heavy-duty applications. However, they may pose more environmental and health concerns due to volatile organic compounds. Both types serve essential roles in aviation maintenance operations.</p></p>
