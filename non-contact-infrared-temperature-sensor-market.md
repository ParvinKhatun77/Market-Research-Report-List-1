<p><p>The Non-contact Infrared Temperature Sensor market primarily features two types: Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors and SMD (Surface Mount Device) Thermopile Infrared Sensors. Through Hole sensors are designed for installation with leads that pass through PCB holes, offering stability and robustness in various applications. In contrast, SMD sensors are compact, enabling a smaller footprint and facilitating automated assembly on circuit boards. Both types provide accurate temperature measurements without direct contact, enhancing their applicability in automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
