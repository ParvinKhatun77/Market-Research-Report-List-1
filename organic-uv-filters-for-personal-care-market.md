<p><ul><li>Water Phase</li><li>Oil Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic UV filters for personal care products are categorized into water phase and oil phase types based on their solubility and formulation compatibility. Water phase filters dissolve in aqueous components, making them ideal for lighter formulations like gels and lotions. They often provide broad-spectrum protection. In contrast, oil phase filters are soluble in lipid-based ingredients, suitable for richer creams and oils, and offer enhanced stability and hydration. Both types are crucial for effective UV protection while ensuring product performance and sensory experience.</p></p>
