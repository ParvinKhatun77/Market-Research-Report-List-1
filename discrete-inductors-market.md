<p><ul><li>Wirewound Coils</li><li>Deposited Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The discrete inductors market encompasses various types, including wirewound coils and deposited coils. Wirewound coils are constructed by winding conducting wire around a core, offering high inductance and efficiency for various applications, such as power supplies and RF circuits. Deposited coils, on the other hand, utilize thin-film technology to create inductors on substrates, allowing for compact designs with lower inductance values and improved performance at high frequencies. Both types cater to distinct needs within the electronics industry.</p></p>
