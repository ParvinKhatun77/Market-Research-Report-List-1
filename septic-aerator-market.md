<p><strong>The Septic Aerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Aerators</li><li>Diaphragms Aerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The septic aerator market is primarily categorized into piston aerators and diaphragm aerators. Piston aerators utilize a reciprocating piston to compress and deliver air into the septic system, ensuring effective aeration for waste treatment. Conversely, diaphragm aerators rely on flexible membranes driven by an air compressor to produce a continuous flow of air. Both types enhance the aerobic decomposition of waste, improving system efficiency, reducing odors, and promoting healthier environmental conditions in septic systems.</p></p>
