<p><p>The Pedal Electric Cycle market includes two main types: Pedal-assisted and Throttle-assisted. Pedal-assisted cycles require the rider to pedal, with the motor providing additional power based on pedaling effort, offering a natural riding experience and extended battery life. Throttle-assisted cycles allow riders to engage the motor without pedaling, using a throttle mechanism for instant power, ideal for effortless commuting or steep terrains. Both types cater to eco-friendly transportation, with pedal-assisted focusing on fitness and efficiency, while throttle-assisted emphasizes convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
