<p><p>Hybrid polymer capacitors are available in two main types: surface-mounted devices (SMD) and radial lead types. SMD capacitors are designed for compact, high-density circuit boards, offering efficient use of space and improved electrical performance. In contrast, radial lead types feature leads that extend from the sides, making them suitable for through-hole mounting in various applications. Both types leverage polymer technology to provide enhanced reliability, lower equivalent series resistance (ESR), and increased capacitance, catering to diverse market needs across electronics.</p></p>
