<p><ul><li>Linear Accelerometer</li><li>Pendulous Accelerometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric accelerator market comprises key types, including linear accelerometers and pendulous accelerometers. Linear accelerometers measure acceleration along a single axis, utilizing piezoelectric materials to convert mechanical motion into electrical signals, making them suitable for applications in automotive and industrial settings. Pendulous accelerometers, on the other hand, rely on a suspended mass that moves in response to acceleration, offering higher sensitivity and stability, often used in aerospace and navigation systems. Both types play critical roles in motion sensing and monitoring technologies.</p></p>
