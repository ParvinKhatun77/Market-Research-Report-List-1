<p><p>The Continuous Level Measurement Transmitter market includes several technologies: </p><p>- **Differential Pressure** transmitters measure liquid levels by assessing pressure differences.</p><p>- **Guided Wave Radar** utilizes microwave pulses along a probe to determine levels in a vessel.</p><p>- **Non-contact Radar** measures levels by sending radar signals that reflect off the media’s surface.</p><p>- **Ultrasonic** uses sound waves to gauge distance to a liquid surface.</p><p>- **Capacitance** measures changes in electrical capacitance caused by level variations.</p><p>- **Other** includes emerging technologies and niche applications that complement these types.</p></p>
