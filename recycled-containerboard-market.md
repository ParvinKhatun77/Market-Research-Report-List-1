<p><p>The recycled containerboard market primarily consists of two main types: linerboard and corrugating medium. Linerboard is the flat, outer layer used to create boxes, providing strength and protection, while corrugating medium is the fluted inner layer that provides structural integrity and cushioning. Both are produced from recycled paper materials, emphasizing sustainability. The demand for these products is driven by the growing preference for eco-friendly packaging solutions, contributing to a circular economy and reducing landfill waste.</p></p>
