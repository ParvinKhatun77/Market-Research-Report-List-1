<p><p>Remote Patient Monitoring devices encompass various types designed to track and manage patient health from a distance. Vital sign monitors measure critical body functions, while blood pressure monitors gauge cardiovascular health. Pulse oximeters assess oxygen levels in the blood, and heart rate monitors (ECG) track heart activity. Temperature monitors check body heat, respiratory rate monitors evaluate breathing patterns, and brain monitors (EEG) capture electrical activity in the brain. Other devices may include glucose monitors and wearable fitness trackers, enhancing holistic health management.</p></p>
