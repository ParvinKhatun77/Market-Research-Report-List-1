<p><p>Demand Side Platforms (DSP) for programmatic advertising facilitate the automated purchasing of ad inventory. The market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based DSPs are hosted on remote servers, allowing for scalability, accessibility, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for advertisers seeking flexibility. Conversely, on-premise DSPs are installed on local servers, offering greater control and customization, which can benefit organizations with specific data privacy requirements or unique operational needs. Both types aim to optimize ad spend and maximize engagement.</p></p>
