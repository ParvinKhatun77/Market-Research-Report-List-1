<p><p>Ceramic fabrication services encompass various techniques for shaping and processing ceramics. Casting involves pouring liquid ceramic into molds to form precise shapes. Grinding is used to achieve fine finishes or specific dimensions by removing material from the ceramic surface. Injection molding allows for mass production by injecting a ceramic slurry into molds under pressure. Machining involves using tools to cut and shape ceramic materials for exact specifications. Other methods may include additive manufacturing and sintering, broadening the scope of ceramic creation.</p></p>
