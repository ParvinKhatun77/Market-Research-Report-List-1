<p><p>The Diaphragm Pacing Therapy System market comprises two primary types: External Diaphragm Pacemakers and Diaphragm Pacemakers. External Diaphragm Pacemakers are non-invasive devices that stimulate the diaphragm through the skin, offering flexibility and ease of use for patients with respiratory issues. In contrast, Diaphragm Pacemakers are implanted devices providing direct stimulation to the diaphragm, offering more permanent and efficient respiratory support.
