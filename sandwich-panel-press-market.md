<p><ul><li>Discontinuous</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sandwich panel press market can be divided into two main types: discontinuous and continuous. Discontinuous systems involve batch production, processing panels one at a time, which allows for flexibility in design but may result in slower output. Continuous systems, on the other hand, operate in a streamlined flow, producing panels continuously, which enhances efficiency and increases throughput. Each type caters to different production needs, with discontinuous ideal for custom projects and continuous better suited for large-scale manufacturing.</p></p>
