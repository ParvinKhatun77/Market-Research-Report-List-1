<p><p>The paper and paperboard packaging market encompasses various types catering to different needs. Solid Bleached Sulfate (SBS) is used for high-end graphics and food packaging due to its excellent printability and brightness. Coated Unbleached Kraft (CUK) offers strength and sturdiness, ideal for retail and industrial uses. Folding Box Board (FBB) is lightweight and suitable for consumer products. White Line Chipboard (WLC) is cost-effective for basic packaging. Glassine and Greaseproof Paper resist moisture and grease, while Label Paper is tailored for labeling applications.</p></p>
