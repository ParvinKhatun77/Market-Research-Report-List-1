<p><p>The phenol-free thermal paper market is segmented into two main types: with top coating and without top coating. Top-coated thermal paper features a protective layer that enhances durability, print quality, and resistance to environmental factors, making it suitable for applications like receipts and labels. In contrast, no top coating thermal paper lacks this extra layer, which can result in lower cost but reduced longevity and print clarity. Both types cater to various industries depending on specific usage requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
