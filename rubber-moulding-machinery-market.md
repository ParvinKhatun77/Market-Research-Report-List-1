<p><p>The rubber moulding machinery market consists of two primary types: semi-automatic and automatic rubber moulding machinery. In contrast, automatic rubber moulding machinery operates with minimal manual input, utilizing advanced technology for efficiency, precision, and higher output. Both types cater to different production needs, with semi-automatic machines typically suited for smaller batches and custom jobs, while automatic machines excel in high-volume production environments.</p></p>
