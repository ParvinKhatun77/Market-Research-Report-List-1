<p><p>The full-size mobile C-arms market is categorized into two main types based on fluoroscopy technology: Full-size Mobile C-arms with Pulsed Fluoroscopy and Full-size Mobile C-arms with Continuous Fluoroscopy. Pulsed fluoroscopy systems emit radiation in short bursts, reducing exposure while maintaining image quality, making them popular for procedures requiring safety. In contrast, continuous fluoroscopy systems provide a steady stream of images, which are ideal for real-time imaging during surgeries, offering enhanced visualization but with higher radiation exposure.</p></p>
