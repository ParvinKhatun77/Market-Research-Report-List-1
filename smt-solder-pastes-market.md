<p><strong>The SMT Solder Pastes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lead-bearing SMT Solder Pastes</li><li>Lead-free SMT Solder Pastes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMT solder pastes market is primarily divided into two types: lead-bearing and lead-free solder pastes. Lead-bearing SMT solder pastes contain tin-lead alloy formulations, offering strong soldering properties and good thermal performance, making them suitable for various electronic applications. In contrast, lead-free SMT solder pastes, which utilize alternative alloys like tin-silver-copper, are increasingly favored due to environmental regulations and health concerns associated with lead. This shift supports the growing demand for lead-free solutions in the electronics industry.</p></p>
