<p><p>The air-cooled screw chiller market can be categorized into two primary types based on adjustment mechanisms: electrodeless adjustment and sectional adjustment. Electrodeless adjustment allows for continuous modulation of the cooling capacity, enhancing energy efficiency and maintaining optimal performance across varying loads. In contrast, sectional adjustment divides the chiller into distinct sections, allowing for staggered operation and maintenance, improving system reliability. Both types aim to optimize energy usage and adaptability to varying cooling demands in commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
