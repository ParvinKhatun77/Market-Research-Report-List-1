<p><p>Semiconductor-grade nitric acid is categorized into four primary types based on purity and intended application. EL Grade (Electronic Grade) offers the highest purity, suitable for critical applications in semiconductor manufacturing. VL Grade (Very Low Grade) has slightly lower purity, used for less sensitive processes. UL Grade (Ultra Low Grade) is designed for specific semiconductor processes where impurities are minimally tolerated. SL Grade (Standard Grade) has the lowest purity level of the semiconductor grades and is used in broader applications with fewer restrictions.</p></p>
