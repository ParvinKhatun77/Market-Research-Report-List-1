<p><p>The Extruded Polystyrene Board market comprises various types, each tailored for specific applications. SS Flat Head Type features a flat surface suitable for standard installations. Si Type (Lap Joint) includes overlapping edges, enhancing thermal efficiency in joints. TG Type (Birch Groove) is designed with tongue and groove edges, promoting a seamless fit that reduces thermal bridging. RC Type (Rain Rrough) boasts a specialized profile for enhanced water drainage, making it ideal for roofing and insulation in moisture-prone areas. These variations cater to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
