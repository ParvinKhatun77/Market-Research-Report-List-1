<p><p>The RTD (PT100) sensor market is primarily segmented into two types: Wire-Wound RTD and Thin Film RTD. Wire-Wound RTDs consist of a fine wire coil wrapped around a ceramic or glass core, offering high accuracy and stability, making them ideal for precise temperature measurements in industrial applications. Thin Film RTDs, on the other hand, utilize a thin layer of metal deposited on a substrate, providing compact size, faster response times, and cost-effectiveness, suitable for consumer electronics and HVAC systems.</p></p>
