<p><strong>The Car Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial (Sliding Armature)</li><li>Coaxial (Sliding Gear)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car starter market includes two main types: Axial (Sliding Armature) and Coaxial (Sliding Gear). Axial starters use a sliding armature mechanism, where the motor's armature moves linearly to engage the flywheel, offering compact design and efficient power transfer. Coaxial starters feature a sliding gear system, where the pinion gear moves along the motor shaft to mesh with the flywheel, providing smoother engagement and durability. Both types aim to deliver reliable engine ignition, with axial being space-efficient and coaxial excelling in performance and longevity.</p></p>
