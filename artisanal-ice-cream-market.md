<p><p>The artisanal ice cream market is divided into two main types: soft ice cream and hard ice cream. Soft ice cream is made using a high air content, resulting in a creamy texture that's served directly from the machine, offering a lighter, more indulgent experience. In contrast, hard ice cream has a denser consistency, produced through a more extensive freezing process and requiring scooping. Both types emphasize quality ingredients and unique flavors, catering to diverse consumer preferences for a premium dessert experience.</p></p>
