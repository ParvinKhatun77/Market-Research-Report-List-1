<p><p>The bioactive materials market is categorized into three main types: powders, moldable forms, and granules. Powders are fine particles that facilitate easy mixing and application in various biomedical applications. Moldable materials are versatile and can be shaped as needed, making them suitable for customized implants or scaffolds. Granules consist of small particles that allow for effective packing and controlled release of bioactive agents, often used in tissue engineering and regenerative medicine applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in enhancing biocompatibility and functionality.</p></p>
