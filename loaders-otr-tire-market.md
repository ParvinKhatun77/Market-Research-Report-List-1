<p><strong>The Loaders OTR Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Loaders OTR Tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature cord layers arranged at a 90-degree angle to the tread, offering superior performance, improved traction, and better heat dissipation, making them ideal for heavy machinery and prolonged use. Bias tires, with layers crisscrossing at angles, provide robust sidewall strength and durability, but can lead to increased rolling resistance and heat buildup. Each type caters to specific operational needs in loader applications.</p></p>
