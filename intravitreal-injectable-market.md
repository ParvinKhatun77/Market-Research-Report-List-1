<p><strong>The Intravitreal Injectable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anti-VEGF</li><li>Steroids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravitreal injectable market comprises primarily two types: Anti-VEGF and steroids. Anti-VEGF agents are used to inhibit vascular endothelial growth factor, which plays a key role in conditions like wet age-related macular degeneration, reducing abnormal blood vessel growth. Steroids, on the other hand, are utilized to reduce inflammation and manage conditions such as diabetic macular edema. Both types are delivered directly into the vitreous cavity of the eye, offering targeted treatment for various retinal diseases.</p></p>
