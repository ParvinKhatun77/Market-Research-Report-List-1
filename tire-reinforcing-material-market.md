<p><p>The tire reinforcing material market encompasses various fibers, primarily steel, nylon, rayon, and polyester, each offering distinct benefits. Steel provides high strength and durability, enhancing tire performance under heavy loads. Nylon is known for its flexibility and resistance to punctures, making it ideal for high-performance tires. Rayon offers good tensile strength and is often used in lighter applications. Polyester is lightweight and cost-effective, delivering adequate strength while resisting abrasion. Together, these materials contribute to tire longevity and performance.</p></p>
