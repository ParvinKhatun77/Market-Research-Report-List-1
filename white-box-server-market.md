<p><ul><li>Rack-mount Server</li><li>Blade Server</li><li>Whole Cabinet Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The white box server market comprises three primary types: rack-mount servers, blade servers, and whole cabinet servers. Rack-mount servers are designed to be installed in standard data center racks, offering efficient space utilization. Blade servers feature a modular design, allowing multiple blades to fit in a single chassis for improved density and management. Whole cabinet servers, also known as tower servers, are large systems housed within a cabinet, providing extensive capabilities for enterprise applications and storage needs, ideally suited for high-performance tasks.</p></p>
