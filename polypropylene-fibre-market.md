<p><ul><li>Polypropylene Staple Fibre</li><li>Continuous Fibre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene fibre market can be segmented into two main types: polypropylene staple fibre and continuous fibre. Polypropylene staple fibre consists of short strands used in applications like textiles, automotive interiors, and home furnishings, known for their durability and low moisture absorption. Continuous fibre, on the other hand, refers to long, continuous strands ideal for applications requiring high strength, such as geotextiles, composites, and industrial textiles. Both types cater to diverse industries, driven by their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
