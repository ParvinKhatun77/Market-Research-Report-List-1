<p><p>The perforated film market is divided into two primary types: perforated manual film and perforated machine film. Perforated manual film is typically utilized for smaller-scale applications, allowing users to manually create openings for ventilation or aesthetics. In contrast, perforated machine film is produced using automated processes, offering higher efficiency and uniformity for large-scale industrial applications. Both types serve various industries such as packaging, signage, and automotive, catering to diverse needs for functionality and design.</p></p>
