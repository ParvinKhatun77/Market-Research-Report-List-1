<p><p>The Hardware OTP Token Authentication market is segmented into three main types: USB tokens, SIM tokens, and mini tokens. USB tokens are small devices that connect directly to computers, providing secure two-factor authentication. SIM tokens utilize mobile phone SIM cards to generate one-time passwords, enhancing security for mobile users. Mini tokens are compact devices designed for portability, generating OTPs for users on the go. Each type caters to specific user needs, ensuring robust authentication across various platforms and environments.</p></p>
