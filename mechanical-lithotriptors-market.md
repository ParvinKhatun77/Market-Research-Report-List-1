<p><p>The mechanical lithotriptors market is categorized into single-use and reusable types. Single-use lithotriptors are designed for one-time application, offering high sterility and eliminating the risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for settings focused on infection control. In contrast, reusable lithotriptors are constructed for multiple uses, providing cost-effectiveness and sustainability. They require thorough sterilization between procedures.
