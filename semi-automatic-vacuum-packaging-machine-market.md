<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic vacuum packaging machine market features two primary types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS) machines. V-FFS machines create packages by forming film vertically, filling them with products, and sealing them, making them ideal for solid items and powders. Conversely, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, suitable for larger or bulk products, providing efficient sealing and packaging. Both types enhance product shelf life and reduce spoilage, catering to various industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
