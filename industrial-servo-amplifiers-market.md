<p><p>The industrial servo amplifiers market is categorized into single-axis and multi-axis segments. Single-axis servo amplifiers control one motor and are typically used in simple automation tasks, delivering precise motion and feedback for applications like CNC machinery. In contrast, multi-axis servo amplifiers manage multiple motors simultaneously, enabling complex dynamics needed for robotics, automated assembly lines, and advanced manufacturing processes. The choice between single and multi-axis solutions depends on the application's complexity, precision requirements, and overall production efficiency.</p></p>
