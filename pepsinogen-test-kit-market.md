<p><strong>The Pepsinogen Test kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunochromatography</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pepsinogen Test Kit market primarily includes two types: Immunochromatography and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay. Immunochromatography is a rapid testing method that uses a simple device to visually identify the presence of pepsinogen in a sample, making it user-friendly and efficient for point-of-care testing. In contrast, Chemiluminescence Immunoassay employs advanced technology to detect pepsinogen levels through light emission, offering higher sensitivity and specificity. Both methods are crucial for diagnosing gastric conditions and assessing gastric health.</p></p>
