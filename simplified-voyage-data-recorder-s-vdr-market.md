<p><p>The Simplified Voyage Data Recorder (S-VDR) is an essential maritime safety device designed to capture and store critical navigational and operational data from ships. The market for S-VDRs primarily features two types: Float Free Capsule S-VDR and Fixed Capsule S-VDR. Float Free Capsule S-VDRs are designed to detach and float to the surface in emergencies, ensuring data retrieval. In contrast, Fixed Capsule S-VDRs are securely installed within the vessel, offering a stable, but less accessible, option for data recording and storage.</p></p>
