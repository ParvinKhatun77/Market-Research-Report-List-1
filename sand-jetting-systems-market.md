<p><p>The Sand Jetting Systems market is categorized into horizontal and vertical separators, each designed for efficient sand removal in various industries. Horizontal separators facilitate sedimentation and separation through gravity, effectively managing larger volumes of sand and particulate matter. In contrast, vertical separators operate on a different principle, utilizing buoyancy and flow dynamics for more compact installations. Both types are essential for optimizing production processes in oil and gas, water management, and other sectors, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing downtime.</p></p>
