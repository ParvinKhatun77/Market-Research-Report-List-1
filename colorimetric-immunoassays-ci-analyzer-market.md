<p><p>Colorimetric Immunoassays (CI) analyzers are diagnostic devices that utilize colorimetric detection methods to quantify specific antibodies or antigens in a sample. These analyzers employ enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA) techniques, where the presence of a target molecule results in a color change that can be measured using spectrophotometry. This technology is widely used in clinical labs for the diagnosis of various diseases, including infectious diseases, hormonal imbalances, and autoimmune disorders.</p><p>The Colorimetric Immunoassays (CI) Analyzer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for rapid and accurate diagnostic tools in healthcare settings. Additionally, the integration of CI analyzers with digital health platforms is enhancing data management and improving patient outcomes.
