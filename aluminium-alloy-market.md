<p><p>The aluminium alloy market is divided into heat-treatable and non-heat-treatable alloys. Heat-treatable alloys include the 2xxx series (copper-based), 6xxx series (magnesium and silicon), and 7xxx series (zinc-based), which are known for high strength and corrosion resistance. Non-heat-treatable alloys encompass the 3xxx series (manganese-based), 4xxx series (silicon-based), and 5xxx series (magnesium-based), offering good weldability and moderate strength. Additionally, other specialized alloys cater to specific applications, enhancing performance characteristics like formability and durability.</p></p>
