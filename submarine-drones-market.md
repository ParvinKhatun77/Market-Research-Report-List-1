<p><p>The submarine drones market comprises two main types: tethered and wireless submarine drones. Tethered submarine drones are connected to a surface vessel via cables, providing stable power and data transmission, making them ideal for prolonged underwater operations and detailed inspections. In contrast, wireless submarine drones rely on battery power and utilize autonomous navigation systems. They offer greater mobility and flexibility for exploration and surveillance in various marine environments but may have limitations in communication range and operational endurance.</p></p>
