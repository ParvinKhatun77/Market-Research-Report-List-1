<p><ul><li>Monopolar Laparoscopic Scissors</li><li>Bipolar Laparoscopic Scissors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laparoscopic scissors market is divided into two primary types: monopolar and bipolar laparoscopic scissors. Monopolar laparoscopic scissors utilize a single electrode to cut and coagulate tissue, offering precision in surgical procedures. In contrast, bipolar laparoscopic scissors have two electrodes that allow for simultaneous cutting and coagulation, minimizing thermal spread and enhancing tissue safety. Both types are integral to minimally invasive surgeries, enabling surgeons to perform complex procedures with improved control and reduced recovery times for patients.</p></p>
