<p><p>The soy-based surfactants market encompasses three primary types: soy flours, protein concentrates, and protein isolates. Soy flours, derived from ground soybeans, offer emulsifying and binding properties, making them suitable for food and industrial applications. Protein concentrates contain higher protein content and are ideal for nutritional supplements and food fortification. Protein isolates provide the highest protein levels and excellent solubility, often used in dietary products and personal care formulations, highlighting their versatility across various industries.</p></p>
