<p><strong>The Concrete Vibrating Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Vibrator</li><li>External Vibrator</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete vibrating machine market is categorized into three main types: internal vibrators, external vibrators, and others. Internal vibrators are designed to be immersed directly into the concrete mix, ensuring uniform consolidation. External vibrators are mounted on the formwork and provide external vibrations to facilitate settling and eliminate air pockets. "Others" may include specialized or less common vibrating devices, such as table vibrators or handheld units, which cater to specific applications in the concrete construction industry.</p></p>
