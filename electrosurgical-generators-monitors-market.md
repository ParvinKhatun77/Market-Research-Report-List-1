<p><strong>The Electrosurgical Generators & Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Vessel Sealing Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrosurgical generators and monitors are crucial in surgical procedures for precise tissue cutting and coagulation. The monopole electrosurgical generator uses a single active electrode and requires a grounding pad, effective for large tissue areas. The bipolar electrosurgical generator utilizes two electrodes, allowing for more localized treatment with reduced thermal spread, which is ideal for delicate surgeries. Vessel sealing generators are specialized for securing blood vessels using heat and pressure, providing efficient hemostasis and minimizing bleeding during procedures.</p></p>
