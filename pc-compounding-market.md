<p><ul><li>PC/ABS</li><li>PC/PBT</li><li>PC/ASA</li><li>PC/PMMA</li><li>PC/PET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PC compounding market types include various blends of polycarbonate (PC) with other polymers to enhance properties. PC/ABS combines the strength of PC with the impact resistance of acrylonitrile butadiene styrene, while PC/PBT offers improved thermal stability and chemical resistance. PC/ASA incorporates styrene-acrylonitrile for UV resistance, ideal for outdoor applications. PC/PMMA enhances clarity and scratch resistance, useful in optical applications, and PC/PET blends provide better recycling options and mechanical performance, making them suitable for various industrial and consumer products.</p></p>
