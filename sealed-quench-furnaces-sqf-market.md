<p><p>Sealed Quench Furnaces (SQFs) are specialized thermal processing systems used primarily for hardening and tempering metals. The market features two main types: Straight Through Type and In-Out Type. The Straight Through Type allows continuous loading and unloading, optimizing production efficiency and reducing cycle times. In contrast, the In-Out Type facilitates batch processing, allowing greater control over material temperatures and quenching times. Both types cater to distinct manufacturing needs, influencing their application across various industries, including automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
