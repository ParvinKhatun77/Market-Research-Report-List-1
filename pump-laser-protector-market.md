<p><p>The Pump Laser Protector market is categorized into two main types: Singlemode and Multimode. Singlemode lasers are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize signal loss and enhance performance. They offer higher bandwidth and are ideal for high-speed data transmission. In contrast, Multimode lasers support multiple light paths, making them suitable for shorter distances and applications where cost-efficiency is essential. Each type caters to specific needs in telecommunications, industrial, and medical sectors, driving market growth.</p></p>
