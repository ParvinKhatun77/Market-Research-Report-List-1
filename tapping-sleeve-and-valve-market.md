<p><p>Tapping sleeves and valves are essential components used in water supply systems for pipeline maintenance and connection without the need for complete shutdowns. These products are widely employed in municipal water systems, industrial applications, and oil and gas industries.</p><p>The Tapping Sleeve and Valve Market is witnessing significant growth due to increasing urbanization, infrastructure development, and the need for efficient water management systems.
