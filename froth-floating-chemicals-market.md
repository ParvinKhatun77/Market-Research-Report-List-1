<p><ul><li>Collectors</li><li>Frothers</li><li>Modifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The froth flotation chemicals market is segmented into three main types: collectors, frothers, and modifiers. Collectors enhance mineral surface hydrophobicity, enabling separation during flotation. Frothers stabilize the froth, helping to create and maintain bubbles for effective mineral recovery. Modifiers adjust pH levels and control the interactions between minerals and chemicals, optimizing the overall flotation process. Together, these chemicals play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of mineral separation in various industries, including mining and mineral processing.</p></p>
