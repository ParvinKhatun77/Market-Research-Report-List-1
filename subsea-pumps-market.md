<p><p>The subsea pumps market comprises several types designed for underwater oil and gas applications. Helico-axial pumps offer high efficiency for large flow rates, while electrical submersible pumps are compact and ideal for deepwater extraction. Centrifugal pumps are known for their simplicity and reliability in various depths. Twin screw pumps provide positive displacement, suitable for viscous fluids. Hybrid pumps combine features of different types for versatility, and counter-axial pumps optimize performance in specific flow conditions, enhancing overall subsea recovery operations.</p></p>
