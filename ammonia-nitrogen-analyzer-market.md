<p><strong>The Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Method</li><li>Electrode Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzer market encompasses two primary types: the colorimetric method and the electrode method. The colorimetric method involves the reaction of ammonia with specific reagents, producing a colored compound that is quantitatively measured to determine ammonia concentration. In contrast, the electrode method utilizes ion-selective electrodes to directly measure the concentration of ammonia in a solution based on its electrochemical properties. Both methods are crucial for monitoring ammonia levels in various applications, including wastewater treatment and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
