<p><p>The Homogeneous Light Conveyor Belt market comprises two main types based on their manufacturing processes: calendering and coating. The calendering process involves flattening and shaping the material into belts using rollers, ensuring uniform thickness and flexibility. In contrast, the coating process applies layers of material onto a substrate, enhancing durability and providing specific surface properties. Both processes cater to various industries, ensuring efficient material handling while maintaining the belts' performance and quality under varying operational conditions.</p></p>
