<p><p>The Cotton Nonwoven Fabric market is categorized into two main types: Cross Layer Type and Parallel Type. </p><p>Cross Layer Type involves the arrangement of fibers in multiple overlapping layers, enhancing strength and durability, ideal for applications like hygiene products and medical textiles. </p><p>In contrast, the Parallel Type features fibers aligned in a single direction, providing uniformity and consistent properties, often used in agricultural and industrial applications. These distinctions cater to diverse needs in various industries, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
