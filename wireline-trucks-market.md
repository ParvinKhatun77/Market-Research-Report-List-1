<p><p>Wireline trucks are specialized vehicles used in the oil and gas industry for downhole operations. E-line trucks are equipped for electric line operations, utilizing electric cables to convey tools and equipment into boreholes for logging or other tasks. In contrast, slickline trucks use a solid wireline for simpler interventions, such as retrieving downhole equipment or conducting pressure tests. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing wellbore data acquisition and maintenance, contributing to efficient hydrocarbon extraction and management.</p></p>
