<p><strong>The Refractory Gunning Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Powder Mixing</li><li>Wet Mixing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractory gunning machine market consists of two primary types: dry powder mixing and wet mixing. Dry powder mixing involves combining dry refractory materials before they are gunned, ideal for applications requiring lower water content and quicker setting times. In contrast, wet mixing incorporates water or other additives to form a paste, enhancing adhesion and workability, particularly for complex shapes. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like construction and metallurgy, catering to specific project requirements.</p></p>
