<p><p>The hemostats market encompasses various types tailored for surgical applications. Thrombin-based hemostats facilitate clotting through the enzyme thrombin. Oxidized regenerated cellulose hemostats promote hemostasis through a reactive cellulose matrix. Combination hemostats blend different agents for enhanced efficacy. Gelatin-based hemostats utilize gelatin's properties to accelerate clot formation, while collagen-based hemostats leverage the natural healing characteristics of collagen, promoting tissue regeneration and hemostasis. Each type serves distinct needs in managing bleeding during medical procedures.</p></p>
