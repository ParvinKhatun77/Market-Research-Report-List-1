<p><p>Calcium silicate boards are categorized into three main density types: high density, medium density, and low density. High-density boards offer superior strength and durability, making them ideal for structural applications and areas requiring fire resistance. Medium-density boards balance strength and flexibility, suitable for general construction uses. Low-density boards are lightweight and easy to handle, often used for insulation and non-structural applications. Each type serves specific needs based on factors like load-bearing capacity, fire resistance, and thermal insulation.</p></p>
