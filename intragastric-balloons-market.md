<p><p>The intragastric balloon market consists of two primary types: saline-filled and gas-filled balloons. Saline-filled balloons are silicone devices filled with a sterile saline solution, offering adjustable volume and a feeling of fullness in the stomach to aid weight loss. Gas-filled balloons, on the other hand, are inflated with gases like air or nitrous oxide, providing similar effects with potentially less discomfort. Both types are minimally invasive options for obese patients seeking non-surgical weight loss solutions.</p></p>
