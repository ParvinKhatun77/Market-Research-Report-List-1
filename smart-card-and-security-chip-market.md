<p><p>The smart card and security chip market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless. Contact smart cards require physical insertion into a reader for data transfer, making them suitable for applications like banking and secure access control. In contrast, contactless smart cards utilize RFID technology to communicate wirelessly with readers, allowing for faster transactions and greater convenience, ideal for public transport and mobile payments. Both types enhance security and facilitate digital transactions in various sectors.</p></p>
