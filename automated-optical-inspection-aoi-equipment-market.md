<p><strong>The Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline AOI Equipment</li><li>Offline AOI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment is categorized into inline and offline types based on their operational integration in production processes. Inline AOI equipment is integrated directly into the manufacturing line, enabling real-time inspection of printed circuit boards (PCBs) as they move through production, enhancing efficiency and minimizing defects. In contrast, offline AOI equipment operates separately from the production line, allowing for batch inspections in a dedicated environment, which is useful for detailed analysis and rework of specific assemblies.</p></p>
