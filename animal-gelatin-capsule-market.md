<p><p>The animal gelatin capsule market is primarily categorized into three types based on the source of gelatin: bovine, porcine, and piscine. Bovine gelatin is derived from cattle and is favored for its strength and stability, making it ideal for a wide range of applications. Porcine gelatin comes from pigs and is popular due to its cost-effectiveness and good gelling properties. Piscine gelatin, sourced from fish, offers a suitable alternative for vegetarian or halal requirements, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
