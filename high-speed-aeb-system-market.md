<p><ul><li>Forward Collision Warning</li><li>Dynamic Brake Support</li><li>Crash Imminent Braking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High-Speed AEB (Autonomous Emergency Braking) system market includes three key types: Forward Collision Warning (FCW), Dynamic Brake Support (DBS), and Crash Imminent Braking (CIB). FCW alerts drivers of potential collisions, prompting action. DBS enhances braking force when the driver applies insufficient pressure. CIB automatically applies brakes if a collision is unavoidable, reducing impact severity. These systems work together to enhance road safety by preventing or mitigating high-speed collisions, improving driver response, and minimizing accident risks in critical situations.</p></p>
