<p><p>The dental zirconia block market is categorized into three main types based on translucency levels: low, medium, and high translucency zirconia blocks. Low translucency zirconia blocks are denser and provide durability, making them suitable for posterior restorations. Medium translucency blocks offer a balance of strength and aesthetic appeal, ideal for both anterior and posterior applications. High translucency zirconia blocks are highly esthetic and mimic natural teeth closely, making them perfect for anterior restorations where appearance is crucial.</p></p>
