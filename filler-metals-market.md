<p><p>The filler metals market comprises several types, including tubular wires, solid wires, and stick electrodes. Tubular wires are divided into flux-cored and metal-cored varieties; flux-cored wires contain flux for better performance in outdoor conditions, while metal-cored wires offer improved welding speed and quality. Solid wires are widely used for their versatility and ease of use in various welding applications. Stick electrodes, or SMAW electrodes, are favored for their simplicity and portability, especially in fieldwork and repair tasks.</p></p>
