<p><strong>The General Industry Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Water-borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The General Industry Coatings market comprises two primary types: solvent-borne and water-borne coatings. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents as the primary vehicle, offering excellent adhesion, durability, and finish, making them suitable for harsh environments. However, they may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-borne coatings use water as the solvent, resulting in lower VOC emissions and improved environmental compliance. While they may have longer drying times and reduced gloss, they are increasingly favored for their eco-friendliness and ease of use.</p></p>
