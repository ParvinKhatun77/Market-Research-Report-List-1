The Closed Type market involves completely sealed systems where water is reused extensively, reducing external contamination and disease risk. In contrast, the Semi-closed Type market combines recirculation with limited water exchange, balancing operational costs and water quality management. Both types cater to varying scale operations while promoting sustainable aquaculture practices in diverse environments.</p></p>
