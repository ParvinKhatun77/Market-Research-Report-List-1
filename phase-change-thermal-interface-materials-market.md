The market is divided into **Electrically Conductive** and **Non-Electrically Conductive** types. Electrically Conductive PCM TIMs are used where electrical conductivity is needed, such as in electronics. Non-Electrically Conductive PCM TIMs are ideal for applications requiring electrical insulation, preventing short circuits while efficiently managing heat dissipation. Both types cater to diverse thermal management needs.</p></p>
