<p><p>The car immobilizer market can be categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I immobilizers are basic systems that prevent engine start by disabling essential components, typically through a key or fob. Type II immobilizers are more advanced, incorporating features like GPS tracking, smartphone integration, and biometric access. These enhance vehicle security and provide additional functionalities. Both types aim to deter theft but vary in complexity and technology, catering to different consumer needs and security requirements.</p></p>
