<p><p>The electric power transmission transformer market is categorized into three main types based on voltage levels. Low voltage transformers operate at voltages up to 1 kV, predominantly used in residential and commercial sectors for local distribution. Medium voltage transformers, ranging from 1 kV to 36 kV, facilitate power distribution in industrial applications and urban areas. High voltage transformers, exceeding 36 kV, are crucial for long-distance transmission, enabling efficient transport of electricity across vast networks, minimizing energy loss.</p></p>
