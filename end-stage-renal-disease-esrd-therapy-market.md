<p><p>End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) therapy encompasses three primary treatment types: hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, and kidney transplantation. Hemodialysis involves filtering blood through a machine, while peritoneal dialysis uses the patient’s abdominal lining to remove waste. Kidney transplantation is a surgical procedure where a healthy kidney is implanted from a donor, offering a more permanent solution. Each treatment type addresses the severe decline in kidney function, catering to differing patient needs and preferences while contributing to the overall healthcare landscape.</p></p>
