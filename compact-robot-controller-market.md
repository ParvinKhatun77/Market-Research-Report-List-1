<p><ul><li>Multi-Joint Robot Controller</li><li>Planar Multi-Joint Robot Controller</li><li>Coordinate Robot Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compact Robot Controller market encompasses various types designed for specific robotic applications. Multi-Joint Robot Controllers manage robots with several interconnected joints for complex movements, suitable for tasks like assembly and welding. Planar Multi-Joint Robot Controllers operate in a two-dimensional plane, ideal for tasks such as pick-and-place operations. Coordinate Robot Controllers utilize Cartesian coordinates for precise movement along axes, commonly used in machining and material handling. Each type enhances automation efficiency across diverse industries.</p></p>
