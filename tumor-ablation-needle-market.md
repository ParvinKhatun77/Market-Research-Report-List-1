<p><p>The tumor ablation needle market comprises several types, each utilizing different technologies for targeted tumor destruction. Microwave ablation needles employ microwave energy to generate heat, effectively destroying cancer cells. Radiofrequency ablation needles use electrical currents to produce heat for tissue destruction. Cryoablation needles utilize extreme cold to freeze and eliminate tumors. Nanoknife ablation needles employ electric fields to induce irreversible electroporation, selectively damaging cancer cells while sparing surrounding healthy tissue. Each method offers unique advantages in tumor treatment.</p></p>
