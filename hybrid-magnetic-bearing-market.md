<p><strong>The Hybrid Magnetic Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic (EM) Biased Magnetic Bearing</li><li>Permanent Magnet (PM) Biased Magnetic Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hybrid magnetic bearing market is characterized by two primary types: Electromagnetic (EM) biased magnetic bearings and Permanent Magnet (PM) biased magnetic bearings. </p><p>EM biased bearings utilize electromagnetic forces to maintain rotor stability and control, allowing for adjustable stiffness and damping, making them suitable for variable load conditions. </p><p>In contrast, PM biased bearings rely on permanent magnets for magnetic levitation, providing inherent stability and reduced energy consumption. Both types offer advantages in precision engineering applications, enhancing performance and reliability in rotating machinery.</p></p>
