<p><ul><li>Hollow Glass</li><li>Vacuum Glass</li><li>Laminated Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sound insulation glass market includes three primary types: **Hollow Glass**, which features an air gap between two panes to reduce noise transmission; **Vacuum Glass**, which uses a vacuum between panes for superior insulation and soundproofing qualities; and **Laminated Glass**, consisting of layers of glass bonded with an interlayer that dampens sound vibrations. Each type offers distinct advantages in noise reduction and energy efficiency, catering to various applications in residential and commercial construction for enhanced acoustic comfort.</p></p>
