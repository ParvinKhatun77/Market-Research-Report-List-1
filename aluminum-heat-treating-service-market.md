<p><p>The aluminum heat treating service market encompasses various types, including stress relief, age hardening, precipitation hardening, and others. Stress relief involves heating aluminum to reduce internal stresses from manufacturing processes. Age hardening, also known as artificial aging, enhances strength through controlled heating and cooling. Precipitation hardening boosts alloy strength by promoting the formation of hard particles within the aluminum matrix. Other services may include solution treatment and quenching, targeting specific performance characteristics for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
