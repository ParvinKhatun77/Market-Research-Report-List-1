<p><p>The artificial ceramic teeth market comprises various types tailored for specific dental needs. All-ceramic teeth are completely made of ceramics, providing a natural appearance and excellent biocompatibility, ideal for front teeth. Metal-ceramic teeth combine a metal base for strength with a ceramic overlay for aesthetics, making them suitable for molars requiring durability. Other types include porcelain-fused-to-metal and resin-bonded ceramics, offering diverse options for dentists to meet patient preferences and functional requirements in restorative dentistry.</p></p>
