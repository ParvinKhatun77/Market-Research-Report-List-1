<p><strong>The Grid-side Energy Storage Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peak-to-valley Arbitrage</li><li>Stored Energy</li><li>Peak Shaving and Frequency Modulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grid-side energy storage plays a crucial role in optimizing electricity use and enhancing grid reliability. Peak-to-valley arbitrage involves storing energy during low demand periods and deploying it during peak times to capitalize on price differences. Stored energy helps balance supply and demand, ensuring availability when needed. Peak shaving reduces peak demand by discharging energy during high load times, lowering grid stress. Frequency modulation maintains grid stability by quickly adjusting power output, helping to keep the system in sync.</p></p>
