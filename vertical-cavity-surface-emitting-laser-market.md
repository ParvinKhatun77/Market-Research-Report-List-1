<p><p>The Vertical-cavity Surface Emitting Laser (VCSEL) market is divided into two primary types: single-mode VCSELs and multimode VCSELs. Single-mode VCSELs emit light in a single spatial mode, making them ideal for long-distance communication and high-precision applications, such as in optical networks. In contrast, multimode VCSELs can emit light in multiple spatial modes, which enhances data transmission over shorter distances and is commonly used in data centers and local area networks. Each type serves distinct applications and market needs.</p></p>
