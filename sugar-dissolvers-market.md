<p><p>The sugar dissolvers market consists of two primary types: continuous and batch systems. Continuous dissolvers operate in a steady stream, facilitating ongoing sugar dissolution for large-scale applications, ensuring consistent output. In contrast, batch dissolvers process fixed quantities of sugar in distinct cycles, allowing for flexibility and control over varying production needs. Both systems cater to different industrial requirements, with continuous units favored for high-volume production and batch units preferred for customization and smaller runs.</p></p>
