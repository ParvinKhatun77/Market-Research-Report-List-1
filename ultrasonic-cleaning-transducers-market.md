<p><p>The ultrasonic cleaning transducers market is divided into two main types: screw hole and non-screw hole transducers. Screw hole transducers feature a threaded attachment that allows for secure mounting in ultrasonic cleaning systems, providing stability and effective transmission of ultrasonic waves. In contrast, non-screw hole transducers utilize adhesive or other mounting methods, offering flexibility in installation without the need for threading. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing cleaning efficiency and achieving optimal results in various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
