<p><p>The Vehicle Exhaust Purification System market comprises various technologies, notably the Three-way Catalyst and Oxidation Catalyst. The Three-way Catalyst is designed to simultaneously reduce harmful nitrogen oxides (NOx), carbon monoxide (CO), and unburned hydrocarbons (HC) in vehicle emissions, ensuring compliance with strict environmental regulations. Conversely, the Oxidation Catalyst focuses primarily on converting carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons into carbon dioxide and water vapor, enhancing overall exhaust cleanliness. Both systems play crucial roles in minimizing vehicular pollution and promoting sustainability.</p></p>
