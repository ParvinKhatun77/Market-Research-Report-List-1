<p><p>The oil-free turbomolecular vacuum pump market comprises two primary types: single-stage and multistage pumps. Single-stage pumps utilize a single rotor to achieve vacuum levels, making them simpler and more compact, ideal for basic applications. In contrast, multistage pumps consist of multiple rotor stages, enabling them to reach significantly lower pressures, suitable for demanding environments in industries like semiconductor manufacturing and scientific research. The choice between these types depends on the required vacuum level and application complexity.</p></p>
