<p><ul><li>Anode</li><li>Cathode</li><li>Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery raw materials market is categorized into three main components: anodes, cathodes, and separators. Anode materials, typically graphite, play a crucial role in lithium-ion batteries by storing lithium ions during discharge. Cathodes, made from lithium metal oxides, determine the battery's capacity and energy density. Separators, usually made of polymer films, prevent short circuits and facilitate ion flow between anodes and cathodes. Together, these components are vital for the performance and safety of modern batteries in various applications.</p></p>
