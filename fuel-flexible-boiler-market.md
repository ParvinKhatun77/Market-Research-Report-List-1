<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fuel Flexible Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) boiler</li><li>Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC)</li><li>Multi fuel firing boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel flexible boiler market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) Boiler**: Utilizes a fluidized bed to achieve efficient combustion of various fuels, providing low emissions and high efficiency.</p><p>2. **Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC) Boiler**: Operates at atmospheric pressure and is suitable for varying fuels, promoting uniform combustion and reduced pollutants.</p><p>3. **Multi-Fuel Firing Boiler**: Designed to burn multiple fuel types simultaneously, offering flexibility in fuel selection and enhancing operational efficiency across different fuel sources.</p></p>
