<p><ul><li>Point Detector</li><li>Open Path Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Catalytic gas detectors are critical for monitoring hazardous gases. Point detectors are localized devices that detect gas concentrations at a specific point, making them ideal for confined spaces or specific areas. Open path detectors, on the other hand, scan a broader area by analyzing the air in a defined path between two points, allowing for the detection of gas leaks over a larger expanse. Together, these detectors enhance safety in various industrial and commercial environments by ensuring timely detection of flammable gases.</p></p>
