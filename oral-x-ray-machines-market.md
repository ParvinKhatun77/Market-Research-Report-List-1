<p><ul><li>Analog X-Ray</li><li>Digital X-Ray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral X-ray machines market comprises two primary types: analog and digital X-rays. Analog X-ray machines use traditional film-based methods to capture images, requiring chemical processing, which can be time-consuming but is often less expensive initially. In contrast, digital X-ray machines utilize advanced sensors to capture images electronically, providing immediate results and improved diagnostic capabilities. Digital systems enhance image quality and reduce radiation exposure while streamlining workflow, making them increasingly preferred in modern dental practices.</p></p>
