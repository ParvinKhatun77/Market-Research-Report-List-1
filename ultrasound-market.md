<p><p>The ultrasound market is broadly categorized into diagnostic and therapeutic segments. Diagnostic ultrasound utilizes high-frequency sound waves to create images of internal organs, aiding in the detection and monitoring of various medical conditions, such as pregnancies or tumors. Therapeutic ultrasound, on the other hand, employs ultrasound energy for treatment purposes, including tissue destruction, pain relief, or promoting healing. Both types play crucial roles in modern healthcare, enhancing patient outcomes through non-invasive procedures and accurate imaging techniques.</p></p>
