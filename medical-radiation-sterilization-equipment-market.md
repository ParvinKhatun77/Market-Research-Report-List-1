<p><p>The medical radiation sterilization equipment market includes three main types: E-Beam radiation sterilization, which uses high-energy electrons to eliminate pathogens; X-ray sterilization, employing penetrating X-ray radiation for effective sterilization of medical devices; and gamma sterilization, utilizing gamma rays emitted from radioactive isotopes to sterilize products. Each method is effective in ensuring the safety and sterility of medical instruments, with specific applications and benefits depending on the materials and requirements of the products being sterilized.</p></p>
