<p><p>The Ultra-high Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE) tape market is segmented into single-sided and double-sided tape types. Single-sided tape features adhesive on one side, ideal for applications requiring strong bonding to surfaces without the need for a secondary adhesive layer. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for secure bonding between two surfaces. Both types leverage UHMWPE's superior abrasion resistance and low friction properties, making them suitable for industrial, medical, and consumer applications.</p></p>
