<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Biosafety Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class II A Type</li><li>Class II B Type</li><li>Class III Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biosafety cabinet market comprises three main types: Class II A Type, Class II B Type, and Class III Type. Class II A cabinets offer personnel and product protection through airflow patterns while exhausting filtered air back into the room. Class II B features similar protections but includes a direct connection to an external exhaust system for enhanced safety. Class III Type cabinets provide the highest level of containment, utilizing a completely sealed design for handling hazardous materials, ensuring maximum worker safety and environmental protection.</p></p>
