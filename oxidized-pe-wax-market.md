Modification oxidized PE wax involves chemical alterations to enhance specific properties like hardness or compatibility, making it suitable for plastics and rubber industries. Thermal cracking oxidized PE wax is derived from high-temperature breakdown, providing low molecular weight waxes used in lubricants and polishes. Each type caters to distinct industrial applications based on its unique characteristics.</p></p>
