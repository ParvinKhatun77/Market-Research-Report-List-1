<p><p>The desk air purifier market consists of three main types: passive, active, and hybrid. Passive purifiers utilize filters, such as HEPA, to trap contaminants through natural airflow, requiring less energy but needing regular maintenance. Active purifiers employ technologies like ionization or UV light to actively eliminate pollutants, often providing faster results. Hybrid purifiers combine both methods, offering enhanced air quality by filtering and actively destroying airborne particles, making them versatile options for various environments and air quality needs.</p></p>
