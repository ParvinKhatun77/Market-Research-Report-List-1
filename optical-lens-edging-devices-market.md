<p><p>Optical lens edging devices are categorized into semi-automatic and full-automatic types. Semi-automatic devices require some manual intervention for tasks like lens positioning and alignment, offering a balance between operator control and automation. In contrast, full-automatic devices operate independently, performing all edging processes without manual assistance, enhancing speed and efficiency. Both types cater to various production scales, with semi-automatic models suited for smaller operations and full-automatic devices ideal for larger laboratories seeking high-volume output and consistent quality.</p></p>
