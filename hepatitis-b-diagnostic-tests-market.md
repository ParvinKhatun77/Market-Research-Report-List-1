<p><p>The Hepatitis B diagnostic tests market comprises mainly two types: Enzyme Immunoassay (EIA) kits and Point-of-Care (POC) testing kits. EIA kits are laboratory-based tests that detect Hepatitis B antigens and antibodies through enzyme-linked reactions, offering high accuracy and sensitivity for large-scale screening. In contrast, POC testing kits are portable devices that provide rapid results directly at the patient's location, facilitating immediate diagnosis and treatment. Both types play crucial roles in managing Hepatitis B infection effectively.</p></p>
