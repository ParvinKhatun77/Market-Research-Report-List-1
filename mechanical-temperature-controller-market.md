<p><strong>The Mechanical Temperature Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jump Type Thermostat</li><li>Liquid Expansion Type Thermostat</li><li>Pressure Type Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical temperature controller market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Jump Type Thermostat**: Operates through a bi-metallic strip that “jumps” between contacts to control temperature, often used in heating systems.</p><p>2. **Liquid Expansion Type Thermostat**: Utilizes a liquid-filled bulb that expands and contracts with temperature changes, triggering the control mechanism for refrigeration and HVAC systems.</p><p>3. **Pressure Type Thermostat**: Relies on changes in gas or vapor pressure to regulate temperature, commonly found in industrial applications where precision is essential.</p></p>
