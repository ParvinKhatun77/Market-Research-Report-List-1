<p><p>The Outdoor Sirens market is categorized into three main types: Directional, Rotating, and Omni-Directional. Directional sirens focus their sound in a specific direction, making them effective for targeted alerts. Rotating sirens produce sound by rotating a speaker, emitting a wide, penetrating noise ideal for large areas. Omni-Directional sirens distribute sound equally in all directions for comprehensive coverage, ensuring that alerts are heard from any angle. Each type serves varied applications in public safety and emergency notifications.</p></p>
