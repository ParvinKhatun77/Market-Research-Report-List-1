<p><p>The GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network) equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: Optical Line Terminal (OLT) and Optical Network Terminal (ONT). The OLT is situated at the service provider's central office, managing network traffic and connecting to multiple ONTs in customer premises. ONTs, on the other hand, are the end-user devices that convert optical signals into electrical ones for devices like routers or computers. Together, they facilitate high-speed internet access and efficient data transmission over fiber-optic networks.</p></p>
